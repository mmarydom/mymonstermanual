Midnight Meryl is believed to be a witch who was beheaded during the middle of her sleep. She can been seen at midnight roaming the streets in a bloodied night gown and bag, searching for her missing head. There hasn't been any reported attacks in the areas she's been seen, but those in the area should still be careful nonetheless.
