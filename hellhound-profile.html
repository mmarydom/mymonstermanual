Hell Hound

The following images display children

drawings and real-life photographs

of the monster.

This mysterious and reclusive monster is known to rummage through cemetaries during certain hours of the night. No one is really concerned about it being dangerous as it's sole food source is dead creatures, which is why many gravesites are commonly found dug-out with the body missing or in pieces. The monster is almost never seen but the few that have seen it stated that it "looks like a giant upright wolf with eyes all over it's head."
