This strange creature is said to be a cursed mascot costume that went ballistic at an amusement park. The monster was never seen again after that incident but those who come across it unfortunately never live to see another day. Their bodies are reported to be ripped to shreds. It is unsure if this monster is worn or controlled by a human being, but either way, avoid the area at all times as it is highly dangerous.
